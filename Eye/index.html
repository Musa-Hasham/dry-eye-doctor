Vision for the Future

Welcome to Dr. Bruce Coward & Associates, serving 4 locations in Eastern Ontario in Trenton, Belleville, Brighton, and Picton. Our family has been providing high-quality optometric services since 1949, and we show the same dedication to our patients today.

We proudly offer full-scope eye exams and several vision correction options and treatments. Each of our locations is equipped with the latest technology for advanced diagnostics.

Protect your vision for the future with Dr. Coward & Associates. We welcome new patients at all locations.

OUR COMPREHENSIVE EYE EXAMS

Our passion for eye care shows through our comprehensive eye exams. We use advanced equipment to evaluate your ocular health, including optical coherence tomography (OCT), pachymetry, and frequency doubling technology (FDT).

Finding the best treatment and vision correction for your eye health fuels our motivation. So, visit us for your next eye exam.
